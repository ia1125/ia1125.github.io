Orange Cinnamon in a train

It's autumn. It's cold and windy. You booked a train ticket to the country side, to visit your parents. Embarking on an old train, you grab your thermos from the bag, and enjoy the warmth while staring at the window.
Suddenly, you remember you carried something special with you. Curious, you open your suitcase, and begin a journey in time.

A message from the connoisseur

This was one of the warmest projects I've done this semester. The concept of time travel (blended with geographical travel with the train environmnet) through old cassettes felt very contrasted to the subject of our class. Yet I beleive we were able to bridge old and new mediums very seamlessly in a memorable experience. Feel free to explore this Notion page to find out our group's working processses and drafts!
